Minicraft Squared is a expansion for Minicraft+ made by pelletsstarPL. Expands the game by variety of new content, including thirst mechanic , sprinting and many, many more.
The goal for Minicraft Squared is to make game even better and make it incredible mod.


Minicraft+ was modded by David.b and +Dillyg10+ until 1.8, then taken over by Chris J until 2.0.4, and then by afyber.


Minicraft was originally made by Markus Perrson for ludum dare 22 competition.
